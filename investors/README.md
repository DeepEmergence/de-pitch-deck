## Delaware incorporation — what investors expect

| Requirement | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| **Entity** | Delaware **C-Corp** (not LLC) for virtually all US pre-seed VCs |
| **Instrument** | Post-money **SAFE** (YC standard) or priced seed at scale |
| **Banking** | US business account (Mercury, Brex, Relay) on Delaware entity |
| **India ops** | Wholly owned **Pvt Ltd** subsidiary; transfer pricing + FEMA ODI if Indian residents fund the US parent |
| **Why Delaware** | NVCA docs, Court of Chancery, QSBS, US enterprise procurement, fast close vs India Pvt Ltd FDI clearance |

India-based VCs (Nexus, India Quotient, Blume, BAT, Speciale) routinely invest into **US holdcos** when founders target global markets — Reo.Dev, Postman, and many SaaS cos use this pattern.
